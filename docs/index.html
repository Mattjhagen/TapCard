Features

Everything you need to make a lasting impression

A complete digital identity platform built on modern, reliable infrastructure.

📡

NFC Tag Programming

Write your profile URL directly to any compatible NFC tag. Anyone with a smartphone can tap and view your digital card — no app needed.

◼

QR Code Generation

Instantly generate a high-resolution QR code for your profile. Export it as PNG, share via Android's native share sheet, or display it on screen.

🪪

Multiple Identities

Maintain separate profiles for Personal, Work, and Freelance. Switch instantly and share the exact identity that fits the moment.

☁️

Cloud Sync via Supabase

Your profiles sync securely to the cloud. Access your cards from any device and keep your data safe with Supabase Auth and RLS policies.

🌐

Public Web Profile

Every profile gets a shareable URL at tapcard.app/u/username. Recipients see your info in a beautiful web view, no installation required.

📥

One-tap Contact Save

Anyone who views your web profile can download your contact as a standard .vcf file and save it directly to their phone's contacts.

How It Works

From setup to share in minutes

No complicated setup. No expensive hardware. Just download, fill in your details, and go.

01

Create your profile

Enter your name, job title, contact info, and upload your photo. Your profile is saved locally and optionally synced to the cloud.

02

Program an NFC tag

Tap "Program NFC Tag," hold your phone to any writable NFC sticker or card, and your profile URL is written in seconds.

03

Share your card

Let someone tap your NFC tag or scan your QR code. Their phone opens your TapCard web profile — no app install required on their end.
